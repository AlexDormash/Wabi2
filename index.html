WaBi (蛙币) is a digital token backed by safe products

We place secure anti-counterfeiting labels on consumer products in China and internationally

WaBi (蛙币) can then be used to purchase them

How are WaBi (蛙币) used?

Single-click automatic payment for Walimai protected products (at current exchange rate if the user has insufficient WaBi)

Paying with WaBi grants additional benefits: discounts, improved delivery terms, bonus WaBi points with every purchase

Allows users to buy loyalty points in bulk with discounts

Development timeline

  • Infant formula scandal. 6 infants killed and 54.000 hospitalised in China. Reported 300.000 victims in total. 2008 Chinese Milk Scandal
  • Value of imported fake goods worldwide reported to be 461 billion USD.
    OECD
  • First discussion about counterfeit products in China between the founders.
  • RFID is chosen as the base technology for our future solution. This allowed us to build a solution that was secure, convenient and consumer friendly.
  • Walimai (蛙力买) is chosen as the name.
    Wa (蛙) - frog
    Li (力) - force
    Mai (买) - buy
  • Official start of business operations in the city of Hangzhou, China. Founders celebrate the official setup of the company and ironically get poisoned by fake whiskey.

    Video shows how fake alcohol (beer) is made in China.
  • Walimai founders engage in a strategic turn-around project for a global top-5 infant formula producer from April 2014 until January 2015.
  • MVP of the Walimai technology is developed.
  • Walimai is selected as one of the most innovative companies at the Open Innovations Forum in Moscow. Walimai’s anti-counterfeit technology is presented to the prime ministers of China and Russia: Li Keqiang and Dmitry Medvedev.
  • Walimai engages top producers of baby food and alcohol with its anti-counterfeit technology and service.
  • Walimai wins an innovation grant from Jiaxing city's municipal government and is incorporated in Zhejiang province, China.
  • iOS, Android APPs launched, new version of RFID labels developed.
  • Walimai opens an online shop on JD.com. Customer feedback is highly positive with consumers willing to pay a 20% premium price for the assurance of safety. Our shop on JD.com (京东)

  • Walimai signs contracts with 3 physical baby stores to set up a dedicated 'Walimai shelf' at their locations.

  • PreICO completed, reaching 100% of its goal. First preWaBi (蛙币) tokens are generated.
    蛙 (wa) - frog
    币 (bi) - money, currency
  • First sales at physical baby stores.
  • Activating sales of Walimai protected products through WaBi (蛙币). Consumers use WaBi (蛙币) to purchase products.
  • Expansion of the number of baby shops selling Walimai protected products. Initiate sales of alcohol products secured with Walimai labels.
  • Start of partnerships with producers and distributors of baby food and alcohol products. Adjusting Walimai technology for pharmaceutical products.
  • Expanding usage of Wabi (蛙币) to other areas:
    - Loyalty programs for product manufacturers (ex. 'Airmiles for consumer products').
    - Partnerships with insurance companies. Product insurance.
